# Contributor brief — eCommerce Operator OS

A one-page brief for specialist agencies joining as contributing partners.

## What this is

eCommerce Operator OS is an open library of evidence-first operating playbooks for ecommerce. Free to read, runnable by hand in ChatGPT or Claude, and installable as AI skills. It is maintained by [Perceptiv](https://perceptiv.digital) and powered by [ShopMCP](https://shop-mcp.app), and it is built to be the library operators reach for.

## The deal

You own a specialty lane (retention, PPC, SEO, CRO, feed, analytics). You contribute a few deep plays in that lane. Each ships with your byline and a dofollow link to your site, here and on the [Partners page](../PARTNERS.md), and your name rides along into the installed skill.

Give the method away, keep the credit. The content is CC BY 4.0. No fee, either way.

## What you get

- **A named lane.** "Contributed by [Your Agency]" on your plays, linked to your site.
- **Distribution to your ICP.** The launch, a high-authority backlink, and AI-search citations. The repo is built to be cited by ChatGPT and Perplexity, so when an operator asks "who is good at [your lane]", your name is in the source.
- **Co-marketing** and a small referral circle of specialist agencies who trust each other's work.
- **Founding-contributor status** if you are in early.

## What a play must be

Not a prompt. An operating workflow for one recurring decision, deep enough that a senior operator recognises their own judgement in it. Full standard: [playbook-standard.md](playbook-standard.md). Reference play: [perf-wasted-spend-killer.md](../playbooks/performance/perf-wasted-spend-killer.md).

Every play names the evidence, uses numeric decision rules, includes weak-data vetoes, shows a worked example with real numbers, defaults to read-only, and carries your credit. CI checks the structure so the bar stays high.

## How to contribute

1. Pick your lane and two to four plays you already run.
2. Start from [the template](templates/playbook-template.md). Set `contributed_by: "Your Agency"` in the frontmatter and the `*Contributed by [Your Agency](https://youragency.com).*` line at the bottom. We add you to the partners registry so your link resolves everywhere.
3. Match the reference play's depth.
4. Run `npm run validate`.
5. Open a pull request, or send us the drafts and we open it for you.

## The honest part

Perceptiv maintains the repo and ShopMCP powers it. You are not selling ShopMCP and you are not signing up to anything. You build your own brand on a resource that happens to be well distributed.

Want us to shape the plays with you on a call? Open an issue or reach out.
